THE ELECTRIC SERENADES

A unique blend of indie rock and psychedelic pop!

"Their unique blend of styles is truly captivating and has gained them a devoted following in the local music scene. Frontman Joe Smith's soulful vocals and catchy guitar riffs are complemented perfectly by the tight rhythms and harmonies of the rest of the band. Whether they're playing a high-energy show at a packed venue or a more intimate acoustic set, The Electric Serenades always bring their A-game and leave audiences wanting more."

"Their latest EP, 'Midnight Dreams', showcases their talent and range, with tracks ranging from the upbeat and danceable "Electric Love" to the introspective and emotional "Fade Away." It's clear that this band has a bright future ahead of them, and I can't wait to see what they do next. If you haven't checked out The Electric Serenades yet, you're missing out on some of the best music this city has to offer." - Review by Scott Moriss (The Rockstar Magazine)
